About Me

Hi there Kota! As you may have noticed from the large text above that my name is Patrick Brennan! I'm a recent graduate of Galway Mayo Institute of Technology where I studied Business Computing & Digital Media and finished with a Level 7 Bachelor of Science Degree (Distinction). After struggling for many years to find a vocation I feel a strong passion for, I believe I have finally found it in the form of web design, in particular front-end development.

Aside from web design my hobbies include keeping fit, reading and drumming. Although I am quite new to the web design world I feel as though my passion and desire to learn will hold me in good stead. I hope you enjoy my one-page site and I look forward ti hearing from you soon!
